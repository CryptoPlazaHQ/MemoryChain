# Quality Management System: MemoryChain

This folder serves as the central repository for all documentation related to the quality management system (QMS) for the MemoryChain project. It outlines the processes, procedures, and standards that ensure the consistent delivery of high-quality software and documentation.

## Contents:

*   **QMS Policy & Objectives:** High-level statements defining the project's commitment to quality and its measurable objectives.
*   **Process Documentation:** Detailed descriptions of key development processes, including:
    *   Requirements Management
    *   Design & Architecture
    *   Development & Coding Standards
    *   Testing & Quality Assurance (Unit, Integration, System, User Acceptance Testing)
    *   Deployment & Release Management
    *   Bug Tracking & Resolution
    *   Change Management
*   **Documentation Standards:** Guidelines for creating and maintaining all project documentation, ensuring clarity, consistency, and completeness.
*   **Review & Audit Procedures:** Processes for conducting internal reviews and audits to ensure compliance with QMS standards.
*   **Risk Management Procedures:** Methods for identifying, assessing, and mitigating project risks.
*   **Training & Competence:** Plans for ensuring the project team has the necessary skills and knowledge.
*   **Continuous Improvement:** Mechanisms for feedback, lessons learned, and iterative process enhancements.
*   **Glossary of Terms:** A comprehensive list of project-specific terminology and definitions.

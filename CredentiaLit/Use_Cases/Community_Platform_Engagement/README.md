This folder demonstrates how the **CredentiaLit SDK/App** enhances community and platform engagement by enabling secure, verifiable, and dynamic role-based access. It addresses challenges related to sybil attacks, managing exclusive content, and fostering a truly meritocratic digital space.

## Use Cases within this Category:

*   **Discord Role Gating:** Assigning and managing Discord roles based on verifiable on-chain assets (NFTs, tokens) or off-chain credentials issued by the CredentiaLit SDK/App.
*   **Exclusive Content Access:** Controlling access to private community channels, forums, or premium content based on verified qualifications, membership status, or achievements.
*   **Sybil Attack Resistance:** Utilizing verifiable credentials to ensure unique human identities for community governance, airdrops, or other sensitive participation.
*   **Gamified Contributions:** Rewarding community members with verifiable credentials (SBTs) for their contributions, enhancing engagement and building trust.

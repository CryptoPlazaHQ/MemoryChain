This folder showcases how the **CredentiaLit SDK/App** transforms education and knowledge management, directly addressing credential fraud, student disempowerment, and the challenges of managing verifiable institutional knowledge.

## Use Cases within this Category:

*   **LMS Integration (e.g., Moodle):** Issuing fraud-proof course completion credentials, managing student skill attestations, and enabling portable academic records.
*   **Research Data Provenance:** Ensuring the authenticity and verifiability of research outputs and datasets.
*   **Digital Archive Access:** Managing granular, credential-based access to sensitive institutional archives and digital libraries.
*   **Knowledge Monetization:** Enabling professors and institutions to monetize educational content and resources through verifiable proofs of access or payment.
